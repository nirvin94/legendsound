About the Founder

My name is Noah Irvin.

I have been passionate about music as long as I can remember. I started playing music at the age of 5 and wrote my first song when I was 12.
I attended Dallas Baptist University where I studied Music Business with an emphasis in Recording. I graduated from an accelerated program in which I also earned an MBA in Marketing. In college my passion developed and I found that my true love and calling lies in music production.
I love being in the studio whether its editing projects, mixing or mastering, and I would love to help you with your next audio project!

In keeping with our core values, Legend Sound is committed to excellence in art, quality in production and professionalism in business.
